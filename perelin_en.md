- [cma-es neuroevolution](https://github.com/mlojek/cma_neuroevolution): This project investigated the application of the CMA-ES metaheuristic for training neural networks. While deep learning traditionally relies on gradient-based optimization methods like SGD and Adam, CMA-ES represents a state-of-the-art black-box optimization algorithm known for its efficiency and performance. This research compared the performance of CMA-ES against traditional gradient-based approaches in the context of training neural networks, analyzing the results to evaluate the potential of CMA-ES in this domain.
